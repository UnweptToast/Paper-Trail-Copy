Paper Trail!!! Welcome to our expense-tracking website! With just a photo of your receipt, our platform automatically extracts details and organizes each purchase into categories like groceries, dining, or transportation. Easily view your spending breakdown on a personalized dashboard, track monthly trends, and set budgeting goals with alerts to help you stay on track. You can also export your data to sync with financial apps, giving you complete control over your expenses—without the hassle of manual entry. Simplify your budgeting and take charge of your finances effortlessly!
